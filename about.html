About us


Our Story
OzGlobal was founded with a vision to help international students. Our journey began in 2018, and since then, we have been dedicated to help students get proper guidance.

Our Mission
At OzGlobal, our mission is to help international students. We are committed to help international students in Melbourne. We strive to see the international students excel in their career.

What We Do
Our Website helps students to connect with the peers. We help students get Jobs and accomodation.

Why Choose Us?
With good years of experience in helping international students we are Melbourne's number 1 guide to help students. Students are always at our heart we are student centric. We are continuously innovating to enhance the student experience. As we were all international student once we believe in giving back to community. Hope you have Great Time with Us.
