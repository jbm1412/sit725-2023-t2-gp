OzGlobal is an online platform designed for international students in Melbourne. It offers solutions to common problems that international students encounter while studying in Melbourne. As the first step, the website provides information about some of the top universities in Melbourne to help students decide which one to choose. Additionally, it shares valuable information about Melbourne City. Upon registration, each member will have a personal account where they can share their experiences and ask questions. Other members will have access to view these posts.
